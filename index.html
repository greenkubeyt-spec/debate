How DebateAI works

DebateAI is designed to help you practise structured argumentation. You choose a debate prompt, select a difficulty, take a side, and respond across multiple rounds before receiving feedback.

1. Choose a focused topic

The app checks whether your topic is specific enough for a proper debate. Vague prompts are blocked so the debate stays useful.

2. Pick your difficulty

Easy gives lighter rebuttals, medium gives balanced challenges, and hard pushes back with stronger counter-arguments.

3. Debate the AI

You present your point, the AI challenges it, and you defend or refine your argument over a few rounds.

4. Receive feedback

The AI gives a score out of 10 and breaks down your logic, evidence, clarity, persuasion, and weaknesses.

Debate flow

Round 1: opening argument. Round 2: AI rebuttal. Round 3: final defence. After that, the AI gives a score for logic, evidence, clarity, and persuasion.

Topic safety rules

  • Blocked if the topic is too broad, such as “war”, “politics”, or “life”.
  • Blocked if it asks for harmful, hateful, violent, or illegal advice.
  • Blocked if it targets a protected group or encourages harassment.
  • Allowed if it is framed as a focused policy, ethics, school, technology, or society debate.
